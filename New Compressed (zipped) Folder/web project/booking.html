Book Your Table

Reserve your table at Lanka Daily Foods for an unforgettable dining experience. Review our reservation options below and then fill out the form to secure your spot. We'll confirm your booking shortly.

Reservation Options & Pricing

  • Standard Table (1-6 Guests): No reservation fee. Perfect for casual dining and small groups.
  • Large Group Booking (7-15 Guests): A nominal reservation fee of ₹500 may apply, redeemable against your final bill. Please mention 'Large Group' in special requests.
  • Private Dining Area (Up to 25 Guests): Ideal for celebrations and events. Minimum spend of ₹5000. Includes dedicated service. Please contact us directly or specify 'Private Dining' in your request for availability.
  • Conference/Meeting Room (Half-Day/Full-Day): We also offer spaces for corporate meetings or private events with catering options.
    - Half-Day (4 hours): Starting from ₹3000 (includes basic AV equipment).
    - Full-Day (8 hours): Starting from ₹5000 (includes basic AV equipment).
    Please specify 'Room Booking' and your requirements in the special requests.

All prices are indicative and exclusive of applicable taxes. For bespoke packages or specific inquiries, please mention them in the 'Special Requests' field or contact us directly.
