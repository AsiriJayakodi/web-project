About Green Garden Bistro

Our Story

Welcome to Green Garden Bistro, where passion for authentic Sri Lankan cuisine meets a commitment to fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Our journey began with a simple dream: to share the rich culinary heritage of Sri Lanka with our community, served in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an exceptional dining experience by offering delicious, high-quality food, friendly service, and a comfortable environment. We aim to be a place where families and friends can gather to enjoy memorable meals and create lasting memories.

Our Cuisine

At Green Garden Bistro, we specialize in traditional Sri Lankan dishes, prepared with love and care using age-old recipes passed down through generations. From flavorful curries and aromatic rice dishes to delightful short eats and sweet desserts, every item on our menu is crafted to tantalize your taste buds. We also offer a range of organic and healthy options.
